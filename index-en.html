Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

SIDRO aims at representing the debates arising around the possible interpretations of symbolism in artworks.

Introduction back to ToC

SIDRO goes to integrate the description one could wish to provide of an artwork through the existing ontologies of the domain. It gives the possibility to describe the content and to shift towards the metaphoric level by talking of what the content represents, i.e. the symbolism. The different interpretations that arise from the content and the symbolic level can be described through the ontology, as well as the debates that emerge between them.
